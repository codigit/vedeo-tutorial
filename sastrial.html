Four score and seven years ago, our fathers brought forth on this continent, a new nation, conceived in liberty and dedicated to the proposition that all men are created equal. Now we are engaged in a great civil war, testing whether that nation or any other nation so conceived and so dedicate can long endure.

We advance thinkers owe our present imunity, such as it is very largely to the fact that even those of our present generation who where formerlt quite against us, have nevertheless being moving, if less rapidly or explicitly in the same direction as ourselve. In their heart, they do not believe we are essentially wrong, but they think we go too far, dangerously and presumtuously too far. But what we live for, our sort is to go too far for the pedestrian contingent.
